**Advantages**:

**Disadvantages**:

- Requires more top-of-funnel volume to compensate for qualification friction
- May miss pragmatic buyers who prioritize efficiency over experience

**Verdict**: Your approach is **strategically coherent** for the client type you're optimized to serve. It would be wrong to genericize the experience—you'd lose differentiation without material conversion gains.

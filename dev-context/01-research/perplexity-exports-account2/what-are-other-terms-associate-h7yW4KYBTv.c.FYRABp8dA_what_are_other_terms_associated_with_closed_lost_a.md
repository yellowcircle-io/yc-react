# What are other terms associated with closed lost as part of lifecycle stages, customer journey, sales cycle/funnel, etc

Closed lost is a term used in sales to indicate that a potential deal has ended without resulting in a sale[^1][^5]. This concept is associated with various stages and processes in the customer journey, sales cycle, and lifecycle management. Here are some related terms and concepts:

## Sales Funnel/Pipeline Stages

- Lead
- Qualified Lead
- Opportunity
- Proposal
- Negotiation
- Closed Won[^5]


## Customer Lifecycle Management Stages

- Awareness
- Engagement
- Purchase
- Retention
- Advocacy[^4]


## Sales Cycle Terminology

- Prospecting
- Qualifying
- Meeting
- Needs Assessment
- Benefits Presented
- Proposal Delivered
- Sale Closed
- Testimonial and Referral[^3]


## Deal Stages in CRM Systems

- Initial Contact
- Qualification
- Proposal/Quote
- Negotiation/Review
- Contract Negotiation
- Closed Won[^12]


## Related Concepts

- **Sales Funnel**: A visual representation of the customer journey from awareness to purchase[^7]
- **Sales Pipeline**: An overview of the company's internal sales processes[^11]
- **Customer Lifecycle Management**: The strategic practice of tracking and optimizing each phase of the customer journey[^4]
- **Closed Won**: Indicates a successful deal where a prospect becomes a customer[^6]

Understanding these terms and their relationships helps sales teams track, analyze, and optimize their sales processes, ultimately improving overall performance and reducing the number of closed lost deals.

# What are resources to check sender reputation

Several resources are available to check your email sender reputation:

1. Sender Score: Provides a free tool that rates IP addresses on a scale of 1 to 100, with 100 being the best score[^1][^5].
2. Talos Intelligence: Offers a real-time reputation score for domains and IP addresses, categorizing them as Good, Neutral, or Poor[^1][^4].
3. Google Postmaster Tools: Provides insights into domain reputation, IP reputation, and other metrics for high-volume senders[^1][^2][^4].
4. Microsoft Smart Network Data Services (SNDS): Offers data on IP reputation, spam trap hits, and complaint rates for emails sent to Microsoft-owned domains[^2][^3].
5. Barracuda Reputation System: Provides both IP and domain reputation lookups, categorizing them as "poor" or "good"[^2][^3].
6. SendGrid: Offers a reputation scoring system ranging from 0% to 100%, based on bounce rates, blocks, and spam reports[^3].
7. MXToolbox: A well-known domain reputation checker that provides detailed reports on domain performance[^8].

These tools use different methodologies and data sources, so it's recommended to use multiple resources for a comprehensive understanding of your sender reputation[^1][^2]. Regular monitoring of your sender reputation is crucial for maintaining good email deliverability rates[^4].

# What are some well known lead enrichment platforms

Several well-known lead enrichment platforms are available in 2025, each offering unique features to enhance sales and marketing efforts. Some of the most prominent ones include:

1. Default: An all-in-one platform with real-time data enrichment, lead qualification models, and automated lead routing[^1].
2. Apollo.io: Known for its comprehensive database of over 200M+ contacts and 60M+ companies, offering advanced search filters and marketing automation sequences[^1].
3. Clearbit: Recently acquired by HubSpot, it provides real-time, AI-driven updates and rich customer segmentation[^2].
4. ZoomInfo: Offers comprehensive business data for lead generation and account-based marketing solutions[^5].
5. Cognism: Specializes in European data and is known for delivering accurate, high-quality contact data globally[^1][^6].
6. FullContact: Focuses on identity resolution, helping businesses create a unified view of their customers[^1][^2].
7. Pipl: Specializes in spam and fraud detection, offering unique capabilities in verifying lead authenticity[^1][^2].

These platforms offer various features such as data enrichment, lead scoring, integrations with CRM systems, and AI-powered insights to help businesses improve their lead generation and conversion processes.
